League Votes for Upcoming Season

Each offseason, managers have the opportunity to propose rule or format changes for the upcoming season. Anyone in the league can submit a topic for vote. Adam keeps track of all suggestions and sends out a poll in the group chat so everyone can cast their vote.

  • Any manager can submit a new rule or change for voting.
  • Adam records all proposals and organizes them into the official vote list.
  • Polls are sent out in the group chat to decide what changes to implement.
